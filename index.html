Introduction

Surabiha is a 23-year-old Designer with a deep passion for UX/UI and Web development. Welcome to her corner of the web, where she shares her journey, experiences, and insights. Feel free to join her as she explores the exciting world of design and navigates the adventures of life.

About

A little insight into her journey: Surabiha was born in Sri Lanka and spent 5 years there before embarking on a new adventure in Canada in the year 2005. After initially residing in Markham, she eventually settled in Scarborough, where she's called home for the majority of her life. As the eldest of three siblings, she's had the privilege of witnessing her younger brother and sister flourish into high school students. Her childhood was filled with cherished memories of cozying up indoors with her favorite TV shows, bonding with cousins, and unleashing her creativity through countless art projects. A fun fact about her is her enduring passion for the arts and design, which has been a constant source of inspiration since her early years.

Work Experience

Surabiha's professional journey took shape after she completed the RTA: New Media program at TMU in 2022. Eager to apply her skills and knowledge, she ventured into the world of freelance work, initially through word-of-mouth referrals and mutual connections. This experience proved invaluable, not only for honing her freelance skills but also for cultivating her entrepreneurial spirit. During this time, Surabiha discovered a newfound passion for website design and development, which led her to delve deeper into creating her own personalized portfolio. Leveraging her existing expertise in UX/UI, she collaborated with a skilled web developer to transform her vision into reality. To explore her portfolio website, please visit the 'Contact/Links' section in the navigation. There, you can explore her portfolio and witness the fusion of design and functionality. Additionally, Surabiha was also able to work as a Print and Social Media Manager for a Martial Arts School in Markham, where she curated their merchandise, posters, asset designs, while also using her video editing skills to create short reel videos for their social media posts.

Education

Surabiha earned her Bachelor of Fine Arts (BFA) in RTA: Media from TMU in 2022. Throughout the program, she immersed herself in a diverse array of disciplines, acquiring proficiency in graphic design, social media management, audio/video production, 3D printing, e-textiles, UX/UI concepts, and introductory HTML/CSS courses. This comprehensive curriculum opened her eyes to the myriad possibilities within the realm of design. The program served as a catalyst for her personal and professional growth, allowing her to explore her passions, gain new insights, and clarify her career aspirations. Following graduation, Surabiha took a year-long hiatus from formal education to delve into freelance opportunities, providing her with valuable real-world experience while contemplating her career trajectory. It was during this period that she stumbled upon the Front-End Design and Web Development program at George Brown, which instantly piqued her interest.
